Summary of Our Approach to Privacy

ApplyFlow Manager is designed with privacy as a core principle. Our approach is simple:

  • Your data stays on your device - We don't transmit your personal information to our servers
  • You have full control - You can view, edit, and delete your data at any time
  • Transparent permissions - We only request permissions necessary for the extension's functionality
  • No tracking - We don't use analytics, tracking cookies, or other user monitoring tools

For Microsoft Edge Add-ons Store submission, we've provided these privacy documents to be fully transparent about our data practices, even though our extension doesn't send or collect data from your device.
