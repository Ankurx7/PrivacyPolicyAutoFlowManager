Privacy-First Approach: ApplyFlow Manager is designed with your privacy as our highest priority. All your personal information stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

1. Introduction

ApplyFlow Solutions, Inc. ("ApplyFlow," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have control over your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how ApplyFlow Manager (the "Extension") handles your personal information when you use our browser extension.

By installing and using the ApplyFlow Manager Extension, you acknowledge that you have read and understand the terms and practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection and Processing

2.1 Information We May Collect

ApplyFlow Manager is designed to facilitate job applications by automatically filling out forms with information you provide. The Extension may collect and store the following categories of information:

Category Examples of Data Purpose
Personal Identifiers Name, email address, phone number, date of birth To auto-fill personal information sections of job applications
Address Information Street address, city, state/province, postal code, country To auto-fill address fields in job applications
Professional Information Work history, job titles, employer names, responsibilities, achievements To auto-fill employment history sections
Educational Information Schools attended, degrees earned, graduation dates, GPA To auto-fill education sections
Skills & Qualifications Technical skills, soft skills, certifications, languages To auto-fill skills and qualifications sections
Job Search Preferences Search terms, locations, preferred job sites To facilitate job searching across multiple platforms

2.2 How We Process Your Data

ApplyFlow Manager processes your information solely for the purpose of providing the auto-filling functionality. All processing occurs locally on your device. Specifically:

  • When you input your profile information, it is stored locally in your browser's storage.
  • When you visit a job application page, the Extension analyzes the form fields and matches them with your stored profile data.
  • When you activate the auto-fill feature, the Extension populates the form fields with your information.
  • When you use the job search feature, your search terms are used to generate search URLs for various job sites.

Important: All data processing occurs exclusively on your device. Your personal information is never sent to our servers, third-party services, or any other external systems.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

ApplyFlow Manager uses your browser's built-in storage mechanisms (specifically Chrome/Edge Storage API) to store your information. This data is:

  • Stored exclusively on your device
  • Associated only with your browser profile
  • Protected by your browser's security mechanisms
  • Optionally synchronized across your devices if you are signed into your browser account

3.2 Data Security

While we have implemented reasonable technical measures to protect your information, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. The security of your data also depends on your browser's security features and your device's overall security.

Since your data never leaves your device through our Extension, the risk of unauthorized access via data transmission is eliminated. However, we recommend taking standard precautions to protect your device and browser, such as:

  • Using a secure, up-to-date browser
  • Setting strong passwords for your device and browser profile
  • Being cautious about who has physical access to your device
  • Keeping your operating system and security software updated

4. Browser Permissions

To provide its functionality, ApplyFlow Manager requires certain browser permissions. Below is a detailed explanation of each permission and how it is used:

Permission Purpose Data Access
storage To store your profile information and settings Accesses only the Extension's dedicated storage area
activeTab To interact with job application forms on the current webpage Only activated when you explicitly use the Extension features
scripting To inject the form-filling scripts that populate application fields Only runs when auto-filling is activated
tabs To open job search results in new tabs and detect application forms Limited to basic tab information and operations
contextMenus To add Extension options to the browser's right-click menu Creates UI elements only, no data access
notifications To display alerts about form detection and other Extension activities Creates notification UI elements only
host_permissions To operate on job application websites Activates only when browsing job sites

These permissions are used strictly for providing the core functionality of the Extension and not for any data collection or tracking purposes.

5. User Data Control Rights

ApplyFlow Solutions is committed to ensuring you have complete control over your personal information. You have the following rights regarding your data:

5.1 Right to Access

You can access all your stored data at any time through the Extension's interface. Since all data is stored locally, it is immediately available to you.

5.2 Right to Rectification

You can edit your profile information at any time through the Extension's profile management interface.

5.3 Right to Erasure

You can delete individual profiles or all your data using the Extension's interface. Additionally, you can completely remove all data by:

  • Uninstalling the Extension
  • Clearing browser extension data through your browser's settings

5.4 Data Portability

While the Extension does not currently offer a direct export feature, since your data is stored in your browser, you maintain complete ownership and control over it.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will provide notice through the Extension or by other means as appropriate.

We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about our data practices. Continued use of the Extension after any changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
